ABOUT ME

Hi there! If you’ve made it this far, you’ve gotten a pretty good glimpse of who I am. My full name is Oluwagbemisola Oyeniyi, but I go by Gbemisola Oyeniyi, dropping the Oluwa. I’ve also given myself a few nicknames like Alvy, Godgbemisola, and Manik, though some are escaping me right now. A lovely woman once nicknamed me "Carry Me," which is the English translation of Gbemi. My first name actually means "Carry me into wealth," and I hope the Yoruba belief that names carry power holds true for me, as I do dream of a prosperous life.



At heart, I’m an art girl, but the tech world has had me working hard to fund my dreams over the past three years. That sentence has made my ink run dry, so I’ll wrap it up by saying I’m currently exploring ways to create wealth without all the wear and tear of the capitalist grind. Let’s see where all this thinking takes me in the future.
